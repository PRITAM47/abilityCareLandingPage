Do you have an approved NDIS plan or NDIS funding?

If you have confirmed NDIS eligibility or are already an NDIS participant, Ability Care is just the company for you.

We can provide you with NDIS support services, such as home care, nursing care, housing support, employment support and many other services which are all approved as necessary and reasonable under the National Disability Insurance Scheme.

We cater to all areas of Western Sydney Suburbs.

Our trained staff will guide you and lead you to an enhanced and fulfilling social and community life!

AbilityCare Services

Therapeutic Support

With our experienced team of Specialised Health Therapists, we provide a wide range of therapeutic support to our NDIS participants. Our team works with you to fully attain functional goal of our participants.

Community Nursing Care

We aim to promote and support physical well-being of our participants by monitoring ongoing conditions and direct treatments which cannot be undertaken due to disability

Exercise Physiology and Personal Training

We provide support with tailored Exercise Physiology and Personal Training services to improve your daily life and give you skills to participate in daily routine healthy activities of life.

Plan Management

We have industry experienced plan management team who will analyse and determine what's best interest you and your choice of support needed.

Accomodation

Ability Care will assist you in applying for rental tenancy, help you understand your tenancy obligations, and assist you in achieving your safe and appropriate home for you.

Aged Care

Ability Care provides aged care services for elderly people so they stay independent as they can. We also provide short term and long term respite care when your carer needs a break or is recovering from accident or illness.
